Built with guardrails, not promises

This is infrastructure, not advice. Here's what we do and don't do:

  • We don't custody funds. Money flows directly between you and your investors. We never touch it.
  • We don't give legal or investment advice. We provide templates and tools. You and your advisors make the decisions.
  • Every action requires your approval. Document generation, report sending, access grants—nothing happens automatically without your review.
  • Clear audit trails. Every action is logged. You can see who did what, when, and why.
  • Compliance-aware by design. We're built to help you stay organized and transparent, not to skirt regulations.
